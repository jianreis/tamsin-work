# Summary

Oliver Wyman has a sustained **retail-health / pharmacy-as-front-door** body of
work: "The Future of Brick-and-Mortar Pharmacy" (2022), "10 Ways Pharmacy Services
Will Change Market Dynamics" (Dec 2025), retail-clinic-as-network-hub pieces (2016+),
and the "Patient-to-Consumer Revolution." Core thesis: physical retail health sites
are becoming a **new front door to healthcare**. Reliability **B**. Figures ⚠️
UNVERIFIED (403).

# Key claims
- Retail clinics/pharmacies are "**the new front door to healthcare**"; Oliver Wyman
  projected **≥$200bn** of US health spend could flow to alternative sites of care.
  ⚠️ UNVERIFIED (extract).
- "**10 Ways Pharmacy Services Will Change Market Dynamics**" (Dec 2025): organisations
  that treat **pharmacy services as a strategic asset rather than a downstream
  function** will pull ahead; retail pharmacists will run **clinical protocols** and
  manage complex therapies within collaborative care teams ("top of licence").
- Physical store estates become **integrated health touchpoints** (screening,
  nutrition, care coordination, behaviour change) — "the places where we shop are
  becoming the places where we heal."
- Strong fit with SA: Dis-Chem/Clicks already own dense store + clinic estates that
  can become front doors.
