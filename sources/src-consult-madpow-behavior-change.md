# Summary

Mad*Pow is a US experience-design consultancy whose signature practice is **Behavior
Change Design** — fusing behavioural/decision science, motivational psychology,
population health, and human-centred design to shift health behaviours including
**treatment adherence**. Directly relevant method for the adhere/refill stages of
scripting. Reliability **C** (firm marketing; the underlying science is sound and
maps to the vault's COM-B/EAST page). ⚠️ outcomes UNVERIFIED.

# Key claims
- **Behavior Change Design** integrates "behavioral, organizational, and decision
  science; motivational psychology; population health; and human-centered design" to
  address **root causes** of individual/societal wellbeing problems.
- Mad*Pow partners with health systems, insurers, pharma, biotech, government, and
  health-tech on prevention, condition management, care-seeking, and **treatment
  adherence**.
- Method: identify **target behaviours**, uncover their **modifiable determinants**,
  then apply techniques best suited to shift behaviour (co-design with patients +
  clinicians, evidence-based BCTs, engagement strategy).
- Maps cleanly onto the vault's existing **COM-B / EAST** behavioural frame — a
  practitioner translation of that theory.
