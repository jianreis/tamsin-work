# Key claims
- BCG predicts consumers will increasingly use **chatbots/virtual assistants** to
  answer health questions and manage chronic conditions; AI moves from automation
  enabler to "autonomous partner" — but BCG frames it as **AI alongside humans**.
- BCG global survey of **13,353 consumers**: for basic health needs consumers
  **welcome AI-assisted support and prefer it alongside human interaction** rather
  than instead of it. ⚠️ UNVERIFIED (extract).
- **BCG Personalization Index** (2025): personalisation **leaders** achieve CAGR
  ~**10% higher** than laggards — a general consumer-experience finding BCG applies
  across industries including health.
- BCG view: "Patients expect to be **understood, not processed**" and want
  seamless, relevant, empowering experiences; legacy infra and reimbursement make
  this hard to scale.
- **Honest gap:** BCG's health depth is in **biopharma** (drug modalities, AI in
  R&D, business-model strain) and consumer personalisation — **not** retail-pharmacy
  scripting journeys.
