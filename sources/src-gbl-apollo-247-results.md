# Summary

The most reliable hard numbers in the India set — drawn from a listed company's
results release rather than PR "registered user" claims. Demonstrates that the
omnichannel model (digital front end fulfilled from a dense physical-pharmacy
network used as dark stores) reached profitability where pure-play burned out.
Listed-company primary disclosure → A.

# Key claims
- **Apollo 24|7 daily orders ~75,000 in Q3 FY25** (up from ~61,000 in Q3 FY24).
- **Apollo 24|7 GMV ~₹760 Cr in Q3 FY25** (+11% YoY), ~₹795 Cr in Q4 FY25.
- **Apollo HealthCo revenue ~₹9,093 Cr in FY25** (+16% YoY); HealthCo swung to a
  ₹32 Cr profit in Q3 FY25.
- Mechanism: teleconsult → digital prescription → medicines fulfilled from the
  **Apollo Pharmacy store network** (largest in Asia) as last-mile nodes →
  ~19-minute delivery in metros. **Advent International invested US$300m (Apr
  2024)** into Apollo HealthCo (with a Keimed merger).
