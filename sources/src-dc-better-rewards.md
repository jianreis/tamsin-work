# Summary

Coverage of Dis-Chem's loyalty relaunch: **Better Rewards** replaced the
23-year-old **Benefit** card on 21 October 2025, shifting from retrospective
points to **instant at-till discounts** and tying loyalty into the healthcare
ecosystem (incl. a Capitec partnership). Multiple reputable outlets → reliability B.

# Key claims
- **Better Rewards launched 21 October 2025**, replacing the **Benefit** card
  (which had run ~23 years).
- The existing **~9 million Benefit members** were **auto-migrated**; existing
  points stayed valid for **6 months** after switchover.
- Model: **instant savings at the till** (not retrospective points), incl. ~10%
  off across 140+ brands / 10,000+ products.
- In the **17 weeks** post-launch, Dis-Chem returned **R410m** in savings to
  customers; **+550,000** shoppers who had not engaged with the brand in the
  prior 12 months came in (Capitec tie-in cited as a driver).
- Positioned as part of Dis-Chem's healthcare-ecosystem and market-share strategy.
