# Summary

The radical price-transparency pharmacy: every drug priced as a fully disclosed
acquisition cost + fixed markup + fixed fees, with no insurance or PBM in the
loop. The pricing *mechanism* is the transferable idea; independent analysis
shows it is not always cheaper than insurance, so frame as transparency, not
guaranteed savings. Company page + reputable press → B.

# Key claims
- Co-founded **January 2022** by Mark Cuban and Dr Alex Oshmyansky. Sells
  medicines at **disclosed acquisition cost + 15% markup + a $5 pharmacy
  (dispensing) service fee + $5 shipping fee** — **no insurance, no PBM**,
  direct-to-consumer mail delivery.
- Opened its own **22,000-sq-ft sterile fill-finish manufacturing facility in
  Dallas, TX in 2023** to make medicines in-house (targeting shortages and cost).
- Catalogue roughly **2,000–2,200 medications** (2024-era figure).
- **Caveat:** a 2024 STAT analysis found patients were better off using Cost Plus
  vs their own insurance in **<12% of generic cases**; the model's biggest value
  is **transparency** (and shortage relief), not universal savings.
