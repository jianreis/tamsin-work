## Summary

Pharmacy Direct (est. 2004), part of the AfroCentric Group (which also owns
administrator Medscheme), is a leading nationwide courier pharmacy delivering
chronic medicine to private patients, medical-scheme members and public-sector
(CCMDD) patients. High volume, high reliability. Illustrates the scheme-preferred
/ DSP courier model and the public-private bridge via CCMDD.

## Key claims

- Operating **since 2004**; nationwide courier pharmacy delivering **prescribed
  chronic medication** to private individuals, medical-scheme members, and
  **public-sector programme beneficiaries**.
- Dispenses **>1,200,000 prescriptions per month**; claims a **99.9% on-time**
  delivery record over **1.2m+ packages/month**.
- Member of the **AfroCentric Group**; the retail arm of a vertically integrated
  pharma operation (wholesaling via Curasana, importation via Activo Health).
  Sister company **Medscheme** is a major scheme administrator (>3.2m lives).
- A **service provider to the National Department of Health** for the **CCMDD**
  (Central Chronic Medication Dispensing & Distribution) programme — public
  division has supported up to ~1.9–2.3m patients.
