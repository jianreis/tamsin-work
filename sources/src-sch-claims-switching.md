## Summary

At point of dispensing, a SA pharmacy submits the medicine claim electronically
through a "switching house" (clearing house) that routes it to the scheme/
administrator for **real-time online adjudication**, returning an instant
response: approved amount, member liability/co-payment, or rejection message.
Main switches: **SwitchOn (formerly MediSwitch, Altron HealthTech)**,
**MediKredit (HealthNet ST)**, and **Healthbridge**. Adjudicated payments settle
to the provider's bank in ~14–30 days.

## Key claims

- **SwitchOn (formerly MediSwitch)**, Altron HealthTech's claims-switching
  solution: real-time online service for **immediate claim submission and
  instant adjudicated response**.
- **MediKredit switch (HealthNet ST)** connects ~**9,000 providers** (incl.
  pharmacies) to switch claims online in real time.
- Switches submit claims to the medical aids **on behalf of the patient/
  provider**; transactions adjudicated online with **confirmed amounts
  transferred to the provider's bank within ~14–30 days**.
- **ICD-10 codes**, procedure codes and billing rules change; inaccurate coding
  → claim errors / lost revenue / rejections (per Healthbridge).
- Real-time adjudication tells the member at the counter whether the scheme will
  pay, what co-pay applies, or that the claim is rejected — before they leave.
