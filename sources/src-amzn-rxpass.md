# Summary
RxPass subscription mechanics, the June 2024 Medicare expansion, and the same-day
/ drone delivery build-out. Company figures flagged; no 403s on this angle.

## Key claims
- **RxPass:** launched **Jan 24 2023** — **$5/month flat, Prime-only, unlimited fills of ~50–60 eligible generics** (80+ conditions). **Does NOT use insurance**; free delivery; 24/7 pharmacist.
- Excludes branded, controlled (no Sched II), compounded, complex therapies; originally barred gov-program enrollees + some states (CA, WA, TX until Sept 2025).
- **Medicare expansion announced 2024-06-20** — Prime members on Medicare, "**over 50 million** eligible," 46 states (company-stated, ⚠️ UNVERIFIED). Claimed ~$2B potential Medicare saving (⚠️ company).
- Same-day delivery: ~8 cities (2024) → "nearly half the US population" (end-2025 framing) → company claim **"~4,500 US cities/towns by end-2026"** (⚠️ UNVERIFIED).
- College Station TX **drone pilot launched Oct 18 2023 (≤60 min, 500+ meds)**; **ended Aug 31 2025** (lease) — not a live Rx channel mid-2026.
