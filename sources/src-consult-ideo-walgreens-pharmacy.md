# Summary

IDEO's marquee pharmacy engagement: it redesigned the Walgreens pharmacy experience
to rebuild **trust-based pharmacist–customer relationships**, prototyping a new
store format and helping launch the **"Well Experience."** A concrete,
named, physical-service pharmacy design case — directly relevant to the **in-store
scripting moment**. Reliability **B** (named client engagement, IDEO-reported scale).
⚠️ scale figures UNVERIFIED beyond extracts (403).

# Key claims
- Brief: shift perception of Walgreens from a convenience store to a trusted
  neighbourhood **health** destination ("corner of happy and healthy"), with the ACA
  bringing millions of newly-insured patients into contact with pharmacies.
- Design move: **bring the pharmacist out from behind the high rear counter onto the
  floor** to listen and ensure continuity of care; add **private consultation rooms**
  and a friendly "**health guide**" to help with OTC choices and appointments.
- IDEO built **two full-size prototypes**; the **Well Experience** format launched in
  two Chicago pilots within months. IDEO reports **~750 Well Experience locations**
  (incl. 14 flagships) with ~1,000 more planned. ⚠️ UNVERIFIED (extract).
- Health guide fields questions on drug interactions / OTC differences; classrooms
  host nutrition seminars — pharmacist time reallocated to **counselling**.
