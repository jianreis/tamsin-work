# Summary

frog's healthcare/pharma POV: **experience design is the difference between a drug's
efficacy and its real-world effectiveness**, because effectiveness depends on
**adherence**, which is an experience problem. frog offers a healthcare **CX
Playbook** framework and reports adherence outcomes from its design work. Strong
conceptual frame for SA scripting (adherence = design). Reliability **C**
(firm marketing). ⚠️ outcomes vendor-reported / UNVERIFIED (403).

# Key claims
- **Core POV:** "Experience is the **delta between medication Efficacy and
  Effectiveness**." Pharma "drop[s] the baton in the home stretch" — winning R&D but
  losing effectiveness because the **patient experience fails to drive adherence**.
- frog uses **divergent thinking** to find the innovations "most likely to affect
  medication adherence."
- Reported result: a frog-designed app delivering personalised coaching + data
  insights reached a **~70% adherence rate** and reduced blood pressure over a
  3-month study. ⚠️ vendor-reported.
- frog **CX Playbook**: a set of adaptable CX practices deployable across an
  organisation for more engaging experiences — a reusable framework spine.
