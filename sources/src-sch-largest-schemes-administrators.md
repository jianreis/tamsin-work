## Summary

The SA medical scheme market is concentrated. Discovery Health Medical Scheme
is the largest open scheme; GEMS the largest restricted scheme. Administration
is dominated by Discovery Health and (from June 2026) Momentum Health, which won
the Bonitas administration tender from Medscheme — the biggest scheme-admin
transfer in SA history. Medscheme is the administration arm of the AfroCentric
Group (which also owns courier pharmacy Pharmacy Direct).

## Key claims

- **Discovery Health Medical Scheme** is the largest medical scheme: **~1.36m
  members, ~2.8m beneficiaries** (largest open scheme).
- **GEMS** (Government Employees Medical Scheme) is the largest **restricted**
  scheme, **>2 million beneficiaries**; restricted to qualifying public-sector
  employees. In 2024 it added 35,772 principal members / 119,383 beneficiaries —
  the single biggest contributor to industry growth.
- **Bonitas** had **~731,576 beneficiaries** at end-2024 — second-largest open
  scheme.
- From **June 2026, Momentum Health administers Bonitas** (took over from
  **Medscheme** after a 43-year partnership). The change adds ~370,000 families /
  ~750,000 beneficiaries to Momentum, lifting its admin market share from ~22% to
  ~30% — making Momentum the **second-largest administrator** after Discovery.
  (This is an administration-contract award, not a scheme merger.)
- **Medscheme** (AfroCentric Group) administers **>3.2m lives** across 15 client
  schemes.
