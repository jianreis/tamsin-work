# Summary

Bain's "Beyond the Pill" article argues that pharma/health companies must compete
on the **end-to-end customer experience around the medicine** (services, support,
adherence, digital touchpoints), not just the molecule. Pharma-customer-experience
POV adjacent to scripting; useful as a CX-discipline import. Reliability **B**
(insight piece). Content ⚠️ UNVERIFIED beyond extracts (403).

# Key claims
- The competitive frontier in pharma is the **experience wrapped around the drug** —
  onboarding, education, adherence support, and digital services ("beyond the pill").
- Improving the customer experience requires the same disciplines as consumer
  industries: journey mapping, episodes/jobs, NPS-style loops, and digital service
  design — applied to patients.
- Better experience correlates with better **adherence and persistence**, which is
  the commercial value at stake (efficacy realised as effectiveness).
