# Summary

Earlier (2014) Deloitte report on **moving "beyond the pill"** — using the retail
pharmacy relationship to drive **consumer engagement and adherence** beyond the
dispensing transaction. Foundational to the now-common thesis that the pharmacy's
value is the **ongoing relationship**, not the single fill. Reliability **B**;
older, so treat as directional. ⚠️ UNVERIFIED beyond extracts (403).

# Key claims
- The pharmacy's strategic opportunity is **consumer engagement** — retaining and
  deepening the patient relationship across refills, adherence, and wellness — not
  just transactional dispensing.
- "Beyond the pill" services (adherence programmes, counselling, wellness) build
  loyalty and outcomes; later Deloitte work judged such attempts increasingly
  **successful**.
- Anticipates the shift to **personalisation, integration, and automation** as the
  spine of pharmacy customer engagement.
