# Summary

Dis-Chem's main competitor's chronic-courier offering: **Clicks Direct
Medicines** delivers chronic medication free of charge nationwide (home or
nearest Clicks pharmacy). Procured through UPD (Clicks' in-house wholesaler).
Plus Clicks' April 2026 launch of on-demand OTC delivery. Company + News24 → B.

# Key claims
- **Clicks Direct Medicines:** **free** delivery of chronic medication anywhere
  in SA — to home or nearest Clicks pharmacy. Origin: "Direct Medicines"
  established 1994 (Afrox/Life Healthcare lineage), now integrated into Clicks.
- All medication procured via **UPD** (United Pharmaceutical Distributors), the
  wholesaler in the Clicks stable.
- **Free collection at Clicks pharmacies** nationwide as an alternative to home
  delivery.
- **April 2026:** Clicks launched an **on-demand OTC medication delivery**
  service (selected areas, expanding) — a direct response to Dis-Chem's DeliverD.
- Courier pharmacy is ~**10% of all SA chronic medication** dispensing (market
  context cited by Clicks).
