# Summary

Scale and momentum of **Clicks Group**, Dis-Chem's primary listed competitor, to
size the competitive set. Clicks is materially larger by store/pharmacy count and
loyalty membership; owns wholesaler **UPD**. Press reporting of Clicks' own
results → reliability B (corroborate against Clicks SENS for exact figures).

# Key claims
- **ClubCard:** active membership grew +800,000 to **12.9 million** (six months
  to Feb 2026); cardholders ~**83.7% of sales**. (FY2025: ~12.1m members, ~82%
  of sales.) Far larger than Dis-Chem's ~9m loyalty base.
- **Footprint:** **900+ stores** and **730+ in-store pharmacies** (FY2025), with
  plans to open ~45–55 new stores and ~45–55 pharmacies/year.
- **Turnover:** group turnover +6.2% to **R23.2bn** for the six months to Feb 2025
  (retail +6.4%, distribution +7.6%).
- **UPD** (in-house wholesaler) recovering after a DC systems implementation;
  good turnover/trading-profit growth.
- Reported continued **pharmacy market-share gains** in core health & beauty.
