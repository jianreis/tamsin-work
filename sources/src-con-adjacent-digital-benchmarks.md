## Summary

The digital experiences SA consumers already use daily — and benchmark every new
app against. Rapid grocery delivery (Sixty60), marketplace e-commerce (Takealot),
and zero-fee digital banking (Capitec/TymeBank) have set expectations for speed,
transparency, low/no fees, and frictionless mobile UX. A pharmacy scripting
experience is judged against these, not against other pharmacies.

## Key claims

- **Checkers Sixty60**: SA's #1 delivery app, **>5 million downloads**; **60-minute**
  grocery delivery; **R35 delivery fee**; **58.1% sales growth** FY to Jun 2024;
  2024 FNB App of the Year. Expanded into general merchandise (2024).
- **Capitec**: SA's **largest retail bank by customers — ~24.1 million clients**
  (38% of population, Sep 2025); built on low fees + simple app/USSD.
- **TymeBank**: **10.7 million customers** by 31 Dec 2024; branchless, kiosk + app,
  onboarding in minutes — banked many previously-unbanked.
- **Takealot / Mr D**: dominant marketplace + food/parcel delivery; Pick n Pay
  online (asap! + Mr D) **+42.5%** online sales (45 weeks to 5 Jan 2025).
- Loyalty: Shoprite **Xtra Savings**, **Clicks ClubCard**, Dis-Chem **Benefit /
  Better Rewards**, Discovery **Vitality** — instant, in-app, personalised
  discounts are a baseline expectation.
