# Key claims
- **Script submission channels:** upload on the Dis-Chem **app**; **SMS**,
  **email**, or **drop off in person** at any Dis-Chem pharmacy.
- **Click-and-collect:** submit script online → collect at a Dis-Chem pharmacy
  in **~2 hours** (present order number + ID).
- **Courier:** submit online → **doorstep delivery within 24 hours** (standard
  courier, distinct from on-demand DeliverD).
- **Repeat prescriptions:** Dis-Chem can dispense repeats for **up to 6 months**
  for Schedule 2, 3 and 4 medicines (per script validity rules).
- **Dis-Chem Direct** is the **courier pharmacy** (not a physical store):
  direct@dischem.co.za, tel 011 589 2788 — chronic medicine couriered to the door.
- **Clinics:** baby/well-baby clinics, family clinics, wound-care clinics,
  vaccinations, wellness/point-of-care screening; staffed by nursing practitioners.
- Dispensary positions itself around medication management, on-time supply,
  and chronic-medicine convenience.
