# Summary

Concrete Accenture engagement + trend data. Accenture helped **CCS** (a European
medical-products/pharmacy company) deploy an **AI predictive-analytics model** that
**improved adherence among targeted high-risk patients by up to 50%** — a rare named,
quantified pharmacy-adjacent adherence result. Plus Accenture **Life Trends 2025**
on digital-health expectations. Reliability **B** (company-reported result; treat
the 50% as vendor-reported). ⚠️ UNVERIFIED beyond extracts (403).

# Key claims
- **CCS engagement:** AI predictive model identifies high-risk non-adherent patients
  and targets intervention; reported **adherence improvement up to ~50%** among the
  targeted cohort. ⚠️ vendor-reported.
- **Life Trends 2025:** consumers expect health to be a "cornerstone of their daily
  digital experience" **yet deeply value face-to-face connection** with providers —
  i.e. digital-and-human, not digital-instead-of-human.
- Accenture's broader thesis: life-sciences/pharmacy must shift from
  **product-focused** to **patient-centred, digitally enabled** models spanning
  pre-diagnosis through treatment.
