## Summary

DataReportal's annual digital snapshot (We Are Social / Meltwater data). Widely
cited B-tier benchmark for SA internet, mobile, and social-media penetration.
Used to corroborate ICASA (Tier A) and to establish WhatsApp's dominance and
internet-penetration gradient.

## Key claims

- **45.34 million internet users** in SA at the start of 2024;
  **internet penetration 74.7%** (i.e. ~1 in 4 South Africans not online).
- **118.6 million cellular mobile connections** = 195.4% of population (multi-SIM).
- **26.00 million social-media users** (42.8% of population).
- **WhatsApp is the most-used / favourite social platform** in SA; Meltwater's
  2024 data: **WhatsApp is the favourite app for ~34% of users**, ahead of TikTok
  (23.8%) and Facebook (18.1%). DataReportal also ranks WhatsApp #1.
- Average SA internet user (16–64) spends **>9 hours/day online**.
