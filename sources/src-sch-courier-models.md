## Summary

The SA courier-pharmacy landscape: scheme-owned/preferred and retailer-run
chronic-meds-by-mail services. Common model — a chronic script is loaded once,
repeats are auto-dispensed monthly and delivered free to home or a collection
point, with the scheme funding the medicine (and typically the courier as part
of the DSP arrangement). Roughly 10% of chronic medicine is dispensed via
courier pharmacy. Includes the Discovery MedXpress "order online, collect at
Clicks/Dis-Chem" network model.

## Key claims

- **Clicks Direct Medicines** (Direct Medicines est. 1994; Clicks acquired 60%):
  free chronic-medication delivery anywhere in SA to home or nearest Clicks
  Pharmacy; relationships with **100+ medical schemes**; ~650,000 prescriptions/yr
  (across SA, Lesotho, Eswatini).
- **Medipost Pharmacy**: SA's first national (courier) pharmacy; **free delivery**
  to home or approved points (doctor's rooms, clinics, PEPcell counters);
  ~**730,000 prescriptions/month**; ~400,000 medical-scheme members benefiting;
  customers **not billed for courier or admin fees**.
- **Dis-Chem Direct Courier Pharmacy**: Dis-Chem's courier pharmacy (not a
  physical store); related repeat/pre-pack service "Pack My Meds" and on-demand
  "DeliverD". (Dis-Chem is SA's #2 retail pharmacy chain, 300+ stores.)
- **Discovery MedXpress**: online ordering/tracking/delivery for Discovery Health
  Medical Scheme members with a valid prescription; **deliver to door OR collect
  in-store at any Dis-Chem or Clicks at no extra cost**; same-day collect (3–6h if
  ordered before 15:00). MedXpress network = **1,800+ retail pharmacies**;
  partner pharmacies hold the clinical dispensing responsibility.
- **Courier pharmacy ≈ 10%** of all chronic medicine dispensed in SA; positioned
  as cost-effective and supporting affordable, accessible medicine.
