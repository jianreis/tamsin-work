# Summary

The broader SA retail-pharmacy field beyond Clicks/Dis-Chem: supermarket-embedded
dispensaries (Medirite, Pick n Pay), regional/independent chains (Mopani) and
buying/franchise groups (Alpha Pharm, TLC). Mixed company + press → reliability B
for counts (corroborate Mopani/Alpha Pharm — see Open questions).

# Key claims
- **Medirite (Shoprite/Checkers):** **140+** in-store dispensaries inside Shoprite
  & Checkers supermarkets; first opened 1999 (Checkers Hyper Brackenfell); newer
  **Medirite Plus** standalone health & wellness format (first 2021).
- **Pick n Pay pharmacy:** in-supermarket dispensaries; PnP operates a pharmacy
  network for scheme members (exact pharmacy count not disclosed in retrieved
  sources — ⚠️).
- **Mopani Pharmacy:** prominent independent/regional chain (Limpopo-rooted),
  large-format stores; specific national count not in retrieved sources — ⚠️.
- **Alpha Pharm:** a **buying/marketing group / banner** for independent
  community pharmacies (count not confirmed in retrieved sources — ⚠️).
- **The Local Choice (TLC):** Dis-Chem's own franchise banner for independents;
  280 franchisees (FY2026), with management citing room for ~500
  ([[src-dc-tlc-500-franchise]]).
