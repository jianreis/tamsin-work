# Summary

The two largest independent SA **courier/distance pharmacies** — Pharmacy Direct
(AfroCentric group) and Medipost — which dominate scheme-funded chronic delivery
and are the volume benchmark for "scripting at distance". Company-published
scale figures → reliability B.

# Key claims
- **Pharmacy Direct** (since 2004; AfroCentric group): nationwide courier
  pharmacy serving private individuals, **medical-scheme members**, and **public
  sector** programme beneficiaries. Dispenses **>1.2 million prescriptions/month**;
  **>800 staff**.
- **Medipost Pharmacy:** described as SA's **largest national courier pharmacy**;
  ~**730,000 prescriptions/month**; **>595,000 patients**; free courier delivery;
  specialised oncology/renal/HIV management; offers a **WhatsApp** ordering channel.
- Both anchor on **scheme-funded chronic** scripts; delivery typically at **no
  extra cost** to the member (funded by schemes as DSPs/network pharmacies).
