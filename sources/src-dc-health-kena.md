# Summary

Background on Dis-Chem's move beyond retail pharmacy into a **healthcare
ecosystem**: **Dis-Chem Health** (insurance — medical insurance, gap & accident
cover, e.g. "MyHealth Plus") and a partnership with **Kena Health**, an
app-based telehealth startup offering low-cost text/voice/video consultations
aimed at SA's uninsured. Reputable press + official pages → reliability B.

# Key claims
- **Kena Health** = app-based primary care (text/voice/video consults) at
  below face-to-face consult prices; CEO/founder **Saul Kornik**; raised ~$3m
  with the partnership launch.
- Dis-Chem partnered with Kena to target SA's **uninsured** population with
  affordable digital primary care.
- **Dis-Chem Health** (dischemhealth.co.za) sells primary health-insurance
  products — medical insurance, gap and accident cover — via partners.
- These sit inside Dis-Chem's stated long-term **integrated healthcare
  ecosystem** strategy (pharmacy + clinics + telehealth + insurance + loyalty),
  the same strategy cited as the reason for FY2026 earnings investment
  ([[src-dc-fy2026-results]]).
