# Summary

Trade-press coverage of Dis-Chem's FY2026 results focused on the **wholesale +
The Local Choice (TLC) franchise** engine and headcount/clinics colour.
Reputable national press → reliability B; corroborates [[src-dc-fy2026-results]].

# Key claims
- **TLC franchisees grew to 280 (FY2026) from 240** the prior year; management
  sees room for **~500** TLC franchise pharmacies.
- External wholesale revenue +11.3%, with 16.4% growth from the TLC base.
- Healthcare-ecosystem framing: pharmacy + clinics + insurance + loyalty.
- Corroborating colour (cross-source): Dis-Chem operated **540+ clinics** with
  **~535 nursing practitioners** and **1.2m+ consultations** in FY2025; group
  employs ~**18,500–20,000** people (figures vary by source — see Open questions).
