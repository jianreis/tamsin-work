# Summary

**D-tier consumer-sentiment signal only.** Specific, dated complaints from
HelloPeter (a SA consumer review platform; Dis-Chem TrustIndex ~2.5/10 as
displayed). Individual reviews are anecdotal and unverified, but as a cluster
they corroborate friction themes (stock-outs, delivery failures, dispensing
error) that recur across other signals — useful for as-is pain points only.

# Key claims (each is one customer's account — anecdotal)
- **Stock-outs of chronic medicine:** a customer (May–Jun 2026) could not get
  blood-pressure medicine Coversyl Plus from Dis-Chem — no stock and "no generic
  alternatives available either".
- **Delivery / fulfilment errors:** order placed 1 Jun 2026; one item flagged as
  needing a script, one out of stock; on delivery, the replacement + another item
  were missing and a wrong third item was included.
- **Dispensing error:** at Dis-Chem Galleria Mall (Amanzimtoti), a 93-year-old
  patient prescribed Spiractin 25mg was dispensed Ridaq 12.5mg with a "Spiractin
  25mg" label placed over the original branding.
- Dis-Chem's overall HelloPeter TrustIndex displayed around **2.5** (low).
