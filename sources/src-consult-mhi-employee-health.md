# Summary

The **McKinsey Health Institute** (MHI), McKinsey's non-profit-style health entity,
publishes on population/holistic health, healthcare workforce, women's health gap,
and employee well-being — **not** retail-pharmacy or scripting journeys. Captured
to be honest about scope: MHI's relevance to SA scripting is **indirect** (holistic
health framing, behaviour, equity), not a pharmacy POV. Reliability **B**.

# Key claims
- MHI's 2023 survey of **30,000+ employees across 30 countries** links positive
  experiences to better **holistic health**, innovation, and performance.
- MHI/WEF "closing the **women's health gap**" work: 9 conditions ≈ one-third of the
  gap, ~**$400bn** annual economic return — a health-equity investment-case frame.
- MHI analysed **115 workplace health interventions** to guide employers.
- No MHI piece found specifically on prescriptions, dispensing, or pharmacy
  experience — MHI is population/equity/workforce-oriented.
