# Summary

**D-tier consumer-sentiment signal only — NOT a factual basis.** Aggregated
themes from Dis-Chem app reviews on Apple App Store (id1226396951) and Google
Play (za.co.dischem). Used solely to surface candidate pain points for the as-is
journey, to be triangulated with B-tier research. App-store listing pages
returned no numeric aggregate rating to automated retrieval on 2026-06-13.

# Key claims (frame ALL as "reviews *suggest*…")
- Reviews suggest **frequent login problems**, including false "jailbroken/rooted
  device" errors blocking legitimate users.
- Reviews suggest a **weak in-app search** returning irrelevant results.
- Reviews suggest **payment-processing failures**.
- Reviews report **orders cancelled/reversed minutes before scheduled delivery**.
- Reviews mention **multiple separate places to set a delivery address**, each
  needing to be set individually (confusing).
- Reviews report **crashes/freezes** and a general sense the app "needs a
  complete overhaul".
- Some users do value the convenience of **repeat-prescription ordering** and
  occasional fast delivery — positives exist but are outweighed by complaints.
