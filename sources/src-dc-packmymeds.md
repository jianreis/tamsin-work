# Key claims
- PackMyMeds is Dis-Chem's online **repeat-medicine ordering + reminder** service
  for customers with active/repeatable chronic prescriptions.
- Customers with active or repeatable chronic prescriptions receive **SMS
  notifications** advising them of the service and asking them to **opt in** for
  repeat-script reminders.
- On opt-in: when a repeat is due, the customer gets an **SMS or WhatsApp link**
  to order; meds are **pre-packed at the nearest store**; ready for **collection
  or delivery**; an "it's ready" update is sent.
- Sign-up channels: online at dischem.co.za/pack-my-meds; **WhatsApp "Hi" to
  0860 347 243**; or call the careline **0860 347 243**.
- Purpose framed as adherence: "get your chronic medication on time, every time".
