# Summary

Details of **DeliverD**, Dis-Chem's on-demand ~60-minute delivery service
launched July 2021 and offered via the Dis-Chem app. Official T&C page returned
HTTP 403 on 2026-06-13; mechanics drawn from launch press (BusinessTech, Stuff
SA, Guzzle, IOL) and indexed page summaries. Fee figures dated and may have
changed — verify before quoting.

# Key claims
- DeliverD launched **July 2021** as a same-day, **on-demand ~60-minute**
  delivery service, ordered via the **Dis-Chem app**.
- At launch gave access to 7,000+ products (health, nutrition, beauty,
  cosmetics, electrical); prescription/repeat ordering also flows via the app.
- Coverage: **selected stores only**, within ~**10km** of a qualifying store, in
  **main metros**.
- Hours: 7 days/week — **Mon–Sat 09:00–16:30; Sun & public holidays 09:00–13:00**
  (one source cites delivery 09:00–16:30).
- Standard delivery fee **R35** (from 1 Dec 2021; originally a flat **R60** at
  launch). Bulk orders >10kg incur extra weight/dimension-based fees.
  *(Fees as published; date-stamped 2021 — ⚠️ may have changed by 2026.)*
