# Summary

The patient-side adherence layer that sits on top of Australia's e-script rail.
MedAdvisor turns scattered tokens/repeats into one managed list, with pre-order
("tap to refill") so meds are ready for pickup, reminders, and carer mode — a
template for the "adhere/refill" stages of an SA scripting app. Company + trade
press → B.

# Key claims
- **Mechanism:** app links to the patient's chosen pharmacy and acts as a **digital
  script wallet** (stores e-scripts + paper scripts + repeats remaining in one
  place); **pre-order / "tap to refill"** lets the user order ahead so the medicine
  is **prepared and ready for pickup**; sends refill + see-the-doctor reminders.
- **ASL + e-script integration**; **carer/family mode** (one account manages
  children/elderly parents, with consent).
- Also sells a **pharmacy-facing engagement platform** (MedAdvisor Solutions /
  PlusOne).
- Scale: ~**3.7–4 million** connected patients through **>95% of Australian
  pharmacies**; works with **37,000+ pharmacies** across US/AU/NZ. **FY24 revenue
  A$122.1m (+24.6%)**, swung to ~A$7m EBITDA profit.
