# Summary

Background on Dis-Chem's history and ownership. Founding facts (Ivan & Lynette
Saltzman, 1978, Mondeor) are corroborated across multiple SA business outlets
(Entrepreneur Hub SA, Leader.co.za, BusinessTech), so treated as reliable for
narrative history; financial figures here are superseded by the SENS results
([[src-dc-fy2026-results]]). Reliability C because Wikipedia/secondary; founding
narrative cross-checked, so used with confidence for history only.

# Key claims
- Founded **1978** by pharmacists **Ivan and Lynette Saltzman**; first store in
  **Mondeor, Johannesburg**, started with ~R10,000 capital.
- Operated linked dispensaries, family clinics, wound-care clinics and
  self-medication centres "since 1978".
- Private-label products introduced ~1997.
- **Listed on the JSE in 2016** with 100+ stores and ~R15.5bn revenue at listing.
- Founder family (Saltzman) remained a major shareholder; in early 2026 the
  founders' sons (Mark & Dan Saltzman) sold a large tranche of shares
  (~R642m / ~R37.9m shares) after inheriting a stake.
- Business segments: retail pharmacy + front-shop, and a wholesale/distribution
  arm (CJ Distribution) plus The Local Choice (TLC) franchise model.
