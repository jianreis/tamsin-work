Structure in brief (as #09): journey = mechanically-titled phase spine. Per
phase: **Need** (first person, bolded key phrases) → named first-person
**Activities** per economy behind the insured/cash toggle, written to the
v3.1 register (what I see/do + FELT-benefit line; one job per activity; all
money in one activity per phase; channel texture in the verbs; nothing
locked, nothing stops on silence; consent as an already-granted fact with
stated scope; content never carrier; relevant detail in its relevant
activity; statuses inline; "out of stock" never "short"; humans by phone
when things break; run the concept-visibility check) with **key moments**
embedded beneath their anchor activities (memory-line titles agreed with me
FIRST - propose, then ask; ⭐ for moments of truth; first-order why
highlighted; concepts linked inline to `08-concepts.html`, never pills or
cite slugs) → **current-state pain points** (HARD RULE: title + description
+ verbatim quote, sourced ONLY from the research pages -
`wiki/dischem/pharmacy-complaints-pain-points`,
`wiki/dischem/dischem-social-sentiment-trust-journey`,
`wiki/dischem/store-of-the-future-customer-voice`, plus for acute
`wiki/consumer-context/voice-of-customer-script-journey` and any
acute/after-hours research pages - never inferred from personas, never
manufactured; an honest no-complaint note where the research is silent) →
collapsed **behind-the-line** blueprint panel (cited capability chips, 🔮
reform / ⚠️ assumption flags). Two-register principle with the cover test in
both directions. Moments earn their keep; uneven distribution across phases
is the point.

hyphen ` - `); "in my language" for first-person language references;

`deliverables/index.html`; commit style `vault: <area> - <what changed>`;

merged).
