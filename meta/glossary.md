## Funding
- **CMS** — Council for Medical Schemes; statutory regulator of medical schemes under the Medical Schemes Act 131 of 1998. See [[cms-medical-schemes-overview]].
- **PMB** — Prescribed Minimum Benefit; legal floor of cover every scheme must fund (emergencies + ~270 DTPs + 26 CDL conditions). See [[pmb-cdl-chronic]].
- **DTP** — Diagnosis Treatment Pair; a defined PMB condition paired with its defined treatment (~270/271). See [[pmb-cdl-chronic]].
- **CDL** — Chronic Disease List; 26 chronic conditions schemes must fund (meds + consults + tests); requires member registration. See [[pmb-cdl-chronic]].
- **DSP** — Designated Service Provider; scheme's contracted preferred provider; using a non-DSP can trigger a co-payment. See [[dsp-formularies-copayments]].
- **MMAP** — Maximum Medical Aid Price; reference-price model capping reimbursement for chronic & acute medicine. See [[dsp-formularies-copayments]].
- **Formulary** — scheme's list of reimbursed medicines; off-formulary = co-payment or rejection. See [[dsp-formularies-copayments]].
- **Co-payment** — portion the member pays (off-formulary, above-MMAP, or non-DSP). See [[dsp-formularies-copayments]].
- **Switching house / switch** — clearing house (SwitchOn/MediSwitch, MediKredit, Healthbridge) routing claims for real-time adjudication. See [[realtime-claims-switching]].
- **NAPPI** — National Pharmaceutical Product Index; 9-digit unique medicine/product code used to claim. See [[realtime-claims-switching]].
- **ICD-10** — diagnosis coding standard linking medicine to a fundable condition on a claim. See [[realtime-claims-switching]].
- **Open vs restricted scheme** — open = anyone may join; restricted = employer/sector-bound (e.g. GEMS). See [[cms-medical-schemes-overview]].
- **CCMDD** — Central Chronic Medicine Dispensing & Distribution; public-sector pre-pack + pick-up-point chronic-meds programme. See [[courier-chronic-pharmacy-models]].
- **Courier pharmacy** — meds-by-mail / chronic delivery model (Pharmacy Direct, Clicks Direct, Medipost, etc.). See [[courier-chronic-pharmacy-models]].
- **NHI** — National Health Insurance; planned single-purchaser fund (Act 2023, paused/in court 2026). See [[nhi-horizon]].
- **Health insurance (vs scheme)** — cheaper primary-care insurance product (e.g. GetSavvi); NOT a medical scheme, no PMBs. See [[cash-uninsured-pathway]].
- **MSA** — Medical Savings Account; member's self-funded day-to-day pot on many scheme options. See [[cash-uninsured-pathway]].
