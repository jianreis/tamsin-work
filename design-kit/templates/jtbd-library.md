# Jobs-To-Be-Done library (prescription experience)

A starter set of the **jobs** customers "hire" a pharmacy to do, framed for the
SA future-state scripting design. Use these to anchor journeys, personas and
blueprints. **Method:** [[wiki/ux-service-design/jtbd-and-behavioural-design]].
Format: *"When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [expected outcome]."*

> 🚧 Draft to be enriched with research citations and SA-specific evidence by the
> synthesis pass. Validate against [[wiki/consumer-context/_index]] and personas.

## Core functional jobs
- **Get a new (acute) script filled fast** - "When I leave the doctor sick, I
  want my medicine with minimum extra effort, so I can start treatment and rest."
- **Keep chronic meds flowing without thinking about it** - "When I'm managing a
  lifelong condition, I want my repeats handled automatically and reliably, so I
  never run out and never have to re-do admin."
- **Submit a script with whatever I have** - "When I have a paper script, a
  photo, or an e-script token, I want to send it through the channel I already
  use (WhatsApp), so I don't need a new app or data I can't afford."
- **Know it's handled** - "When I've submitted, I want to know it's valid, in
  stock, what it costs, and when I'll get it, so I'm not left guessing."
- **Pay in a way that works for me** - "When I check out, I want my scheme
  applied automatically OR a clear cash price up front, so there are no surprises."
- **Get it where I am** - "When I can't reach a store, I want collection or
  delivery options that fit my address reality, so distance isn't a barrier."
- **Take it correctly** - "When I get my meds, I want to understand how to take
  them (in my language), so I get better and avoid harm."
- **Renew without a new doctor visit when safe** - "When my repeat runs out, I
  want a fast, safe renewal path, so a script technicality doesn't interrupt care."

## Emotional & social jobs
- **Feel in control of my health, not processed by a system.**
- **Be treated with dignity and privacy** (esp. stigmatised conditions - HIV,
  mental health, contraception, chronic).
- **Trust that the medicine is right and safe** (correct, genuine, checked by a
  pharmacist).
- **Not feel poor or judged** at the point of paying / asking about cost.
- **Manage care for someone else** (child, parent, dependent) without friction.

## Jobs by segment (seed - expand with personas)
- **Insured chronic:** automate repeats, apply scheme, deliver to door, alert before run-out.
- **Cash-pay acute:** lowest hassle + transparent price + nearest fast option.
- **Caregiver/proxy:** manage multiple profiles, collect on behalf, clear authority.
- **Elderly / poly-pharmacy:** simple, reassuring, packaged-by-dose, human help available.
- **Low-data / peri-urban:** WhatsApp/USSD-first, offline-tolerant, pickup-point friendly.

## How to use
For each persona × scenario, pick the dominant job, then design the journey to
nail that job's **expected outcome** while resolving the **pains** that block it
(see [[journey-map-template]]). Rank features by how directly they advance a core job.
