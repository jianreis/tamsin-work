## SLIDE: exec-summary

title: Everyone has mapped the pharmacy of the future - nobody has built the scripting journey for ours
so_what: The end-to-end prescription experience for a regulated, multi-funder, low-bandwidth market is unclaimed white space, and that is the opportunity.
body:
  - Strategy firms own the destination: pharmacy as health front door, top-of-licence, omnichannel.
  - Design firms own the experience: trust, adherence, the script artefact itself.
  - Neither connects them into one journey - submit, validate, dispense, pay, collect, deliver, adhere.
  - None handles SA's reality at once: scheduling law, scheme adjudication, POPIA, WhatsApp/USSD fallback.
  - The blueprint that fuses all three is the asset no competitor has published.
