How to read these
  • Start with 05 — the marquee future-state journey (the experience definition). Then read 01 for the strategy and pillars, and 02 → 03 → 04 to follow the journey from today to aspirational.
  • 05 is interactive: a Journey ⇄ My medicine cabinet view switch, a dual-economy toggle, expandable moments (story + capabilities behind the line), a per-moment “today’s pain” overlay, an emotion / Patient-Trust curve, and a pillar filter.
  • The blueprints (02–04) are interactive: click a stage to expand its lanes, switch between Insured (Nomvula) and Cash/uninsured (Sipho), watch the emotion curve update, hover any underlined text to see its source, and click a pillar to highlight where it recurs.
  • Legend: ⭐ moment of truth · 🔴 pain · 💡 opportunity · ⚠️ knowledge gap / unverified · 🔮 requires-reform. Dashed borders mark anything speculative or requiring reform.
  • Every file is self-contained — open by double-click, works offline, prints cleanly (expands everything for PDF).

Shared assets: the interactive engine lives in _shared/blueprint-kit.html (reference implementation) and the canonical pillars in _shared/pillars.md. Some linked files may not be generated yet.
